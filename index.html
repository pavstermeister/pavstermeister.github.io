Website builder support

Jumpstart your website project

Get your project up and running by leveraging the power of website builders such as Wordpress, Shopify or Wix. We can help you with initial setup, customisation and training. We are happy to work with new or modify existing projects.

Recommended for:

Website builders can be a pleasure to work with if you are happy to rely on built-in features and a pain in the neck if you try to customise them. Website builders can be set up in a very short amount of time.

Benefits:
  • Plenty of ready-to-go templates
  • Extendible using add-ons
  • User editable out of the box
Limitations:
  • Harder to tweak details

Development process

Witness your ideas come to life

Ideation and Wireframing

We try to understand your vision and translate it to a set of actionable development tasks. Formulating a user story and a reason to build an element or a feature. Putting ideas down, either on paper or using wireframing software.

Minimum Awesome Product

Implementing core features of the design. Ensuring the most important aspects of functionality and styling are addressed. Already at this stage we are aiming at providing a fully usable product or a feature without compromises on quality.

Feature Complete Version

At this stage we focus on adding non core elements and decorations. We improve the usability and aesthetics by responding to real world feedback. Your product or feature really begins to shine.
